What is TUFourms?

TUFourms, or The Universal Forums, is the largest and most active community hub for custom levels for A Dance of Fire and Ice. It serves as a central platform where mappers and players can share, discover, and discuss community-made content. While custom levels can be found on other platforms, TUFourms is the primary destination for high-quality, community-vetted levels, making it a core part of the ADOFAI ecosystem.

Key features of the forum include a massive library of level submissions, complete with download links and discussion threads. The site is also home to the TUF Rating System, a unique and widely-used difficulty rating system that provides a consistent way to gauge a level's challenge.

Whether you're looking for new levels to play, want to share your own creations, or just want to connect with other players, TUFourms is an essential resource for any ADOFAI enthusiast.

TUFourms's Ranking System


Tier Prefix Description
Planetary P The "P" tier covers levels that range from relatively simple to a moderate challenge. These levels are great for new players entering the custom level scene and for those looking for a fun, not-too-demanding experience.
Galactic G The "G" tier is where the difficulty significantly increases. Levels in this category require solid timing, precision, and an understanding of advanced mechanics. They are considered the "standard" challenge for experienced players and represent a notable step up in difficulty.
Universal U The "U" tier is reserved for the most difficult and challenging levels in the community. These are often referred to as "universals" and are designed to push the boundaries of what is possible in the game. They require an incredible amount of skill and dedication to complete.
