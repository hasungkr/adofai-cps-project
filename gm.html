Game Mechanics

Gameplay

Gameplay in A Dance of Fire and Ice consists of two orbs known as Planets, one stationary on a track and the other orbiting the first one. The player has to tap the screen or press a key when the orbiting planet is over the next tile on the track, which locks the orbiting planet in place and causes the other planet to orbit in turn. If the orbiting planet makes a full circle around the stationary planet or the player taps too early too many times, the orbiting planet will crash into the stationary planet, causing them both to explode, resulting in failure.
